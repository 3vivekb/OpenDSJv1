Mapping San Jose Campaign Finance Data

Campaign finance data can be complex and confusing -- but it doesn't have to be. One way to make sense of it all is through data visualization and mapping. Learn where San Jose mayoral candidates campaign contributions come from. (Candidates are in order of most funds raised.) This map overlays campaign contribution data onto a map of San Jose zip codes to illuminate the connections between campaign contributors and mayoral candidates.

More Information

What are we mapping?

Mayoral candidates and information on campaign donors.

Who should use this site?

Citizens who are interested in understanding campaign finance contributions and holding thier representatives accountable, if money=influence, knowledge=power.

Why did we make this app?

Our goal is to provide transparent non-partisan campaign contribution data in an accessible and easy to understand format. We hope this site will provide a forum for discussion and feedback as well as provide ways to engage the public and raise awareness and accountability. Ultimately this is one step toward shifting politics into a movemet of civic engagement and ultimate citizen action.
